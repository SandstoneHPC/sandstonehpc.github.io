Schedule a Slurm Job

To schedule a Slurm job using the Slurm Assist form:

  1. Begin by opening the Slurm Assist app.
  2. Select the Profile that best describes the job you wish to run.
  3. The form will be prepopulated with a job template. To extend this template, click the Add Directive typeahead and select the field you wish to add. Form values will be validated as you type.
  4. To get more information about a given field, click the Help button attached to the field.
  5. Once you've added all desired directives to the form, and all values are valid, click Schedule Job and select where you'd like to save the generated sbatch script.
  6. After you save the script, you'll get a confirmation that the job was submitted successfully.

Checking Job Status

To check the status of a Slurm job that is queued or running:

  1. Click the Status tab.
  2. Click Refresh to load the most up-to-date information from Slurm.
  3. A list of queued, running, and recently completed jobs will be shown in the job table.
  4. To view job details, click the Details button on that job's row.
